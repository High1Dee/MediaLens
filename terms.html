Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. No Data Collection

MediaLens is designed to operate entirely in your browser. We do not:

  • Collect or store the links you paste
  • Track your usage or browsing behavior
  • Use analytics cookies or tracking pixels
  • Require any form of registration or login
  • Store personal information about users

The preview process happens locally in your browser. Links are not sent to our servers.

2. Third-Party APIs

When you paste a link, your browser may make requests directly to social media platforms' embed APIs. These requests are made from your browser to the respective platforms, not through our servers.

Platforms may have their own privacy policies regarding embed requests. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of platforms whose content you preview.

3. Browser Storage

MediaLens may use your browser's local storage to remember your preferences (like dark mode settings, if implemented). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

4. External Resources

We use the following external resources that may have their own privacy policies:

  • Bootstrap CSS/JS: Loaded from local files
  • FontAwesome Icons: Loaded from CDNJS

These resources are commonly used across the web and follow standard privacy practices.

5. Children's Privacy

MediaLens is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe we might have any information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Our Promise: We built MediaLens with privacy as a core principle. We don't want your data - we just want to provide a useful tool.
